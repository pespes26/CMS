Welcome to the Course Management System

This Course Management System is designed to help students and administrators efficiently manage university courses, schedules, and preferences. Whether you're adding a new course, editing existing ones, or generating a personalized timetable, this system has got you covered.

Key Features

  • Courses List: View all available courses in a structured format.
  • Time Table: Access your current timetable with all scheduled classes.
  • Add/Edit Courses: Easily add new courses or modify details of existing ones.
  • Edit Preferences: Set your preferences for course scheduling, such as preferred days and times.
  • Generate New Table: Create a new timetable based on your preferences and course availability.

How to Use the System

To make the most out of the Course Management System, follow these simple steps:

  • Step 1: Start by exploring the Courses List to see all available courses.
  • Step 2: Add any new courses you wish to take using the Add Course feature.
  • Step 3: Go to Edit Preferences to set your scheduling preferences, such as preferred days and restricted hours.
  • Step 4: Once your preferences are set, head over to Generate New Table to create your personalized timetable.
  • Step 5: Review your generated timetable and save it. You can view the final version in the Time Table section.

Why Use This System?

This system is designed to streamline your course management process, allowing you to focus on what truly matters: your studies. With features like automatic timetable generation and easy course editing, you'll save time and reduce the stress of manual scheduling.
