Welcome to the Course Management System

The Course Management System helps you manage your university schedule efficiently. You can add new courses, manage lectures and practices, edit your scheduling preferences, and automatically generate optimal timetables based on your choices.

Key Features

  • Course Management: Add and edit courses, lectures, and practices. You can input details such as time slots, buildings, and classrooms.
  • Timetable Generation: Create a personalized timetable by choosing between long days with fewer hours or shorter school days with more hours. The system will automatically optimize your schedule based on your preferences.
  • Edit Preferences: Set preferences for scheduling, including selecting preferred days for classes and restricted time slots where you don’t want any classes.
  • Conflict-Free Scheduling: The system ensures there are no overlapping classes when generating timetables, adjusting based on your preferences.

How to Use the System

Follow these steps to make the most out of the Course Management System:

  • Step 1: Visit the Course List to view all available courses or the ones you've already entered.
  • Step 2: Use the Add Course feature to input new courses, including lectures and practices with specific time slots, buildings, and classrooms.
  • Step 3: Go to Edit Preferences to define your scheduling preferences. You can choose between long school days (fewer days) or shorter days (more days). Additionally, set any restricted times where you don’t want classes.
  • Step 4: Once your preferences are set, use the Generate New Table feature. The system will automatically build a timetable without overlapping lectures or practices.
  • Step 5: Review your timetable by heading to the Timetable section.

Additional Features

  • The system automatically adjusts schedules to prevent overlap between lectures and practices, ensuring smooth planning.
  • You can enter restricted time slots for specific days, and the system will ensure no classes are scheduled during those periods.
  • The timetable will display all the lectures and practices you’ve added, including color-coded distinctions for lectures and practices.

Why Use This System?

The Course Management System simplifies the course planning process, automatically optimizing your schedule based on your preferences and constraints. By avoiding manual scheduling conflicts, this system helps you focus on studying and frees up your time for other tasks.
